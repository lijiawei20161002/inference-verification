READING IT:
  * AUC rises monotonically with distance-from-M: quantized-self ~0.5 (invisible to a
    cheap proxy), different-family ~1.0 (trivially caught). The cheap proxy WORKS once the
    substitution is far enough that its conditional distribution diverges from M's.
  * A DIFFERENT-family proxy (SmolLM2) still detects far substitutions but is weaker on
    near ones -- proxy lineage matters most exactly where detection is hardest.
  * Sub-0.5 signed AUC on the near rows is the noise-fit artifact (mean-NLL AUC stays 0.5).
  * More scored tokens k => higher AUC on the detectable rows: k is the cost/accuracy knob.
